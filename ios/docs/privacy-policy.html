VOXTV — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

Overview

VOXTV is a media player application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No accounts or registration — VOXTV does not require sign-up, email, or any personal information.
  • No analytics or telemetry — The app does not send usage data, crash reports, or any information to our servers or third parties.
  • No advertising — There are no ads, ad networks, or tracking SDKs.
  • No data sharing — No user data is sold, shared, or transmitted to any third party.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally:

  • Playlist URLs and credentials — Server addresses and login credentials you provide for IPTV services are stored locally in an encrypted database on your device.
  • Favorites and watch history — Your channel favorites and viewing history are stored locally.
  • App settings — Theme, language, and playback preferences are stored locally.
  • Channel logos cache — Downloaded logos are cached locally to improve performance.

Network Access

VOXTV connects to the internet for the following purposes only:

  • Streaming content — Connecting to IPTV servers you configure to play media streams.
  • Playlist and EPG downloading — Fetching playlist files and electronic program guide data from URLs you provide.
  • Channel logos — Downloading channel artwork from URLs specified in your playlist.
  • Movie metadata — Fetching movie/series information from OMDb API for VOD content display.

Local Network Access

VOXTV may use your local Wi-Fi network for:

  • QR Code playlist import — A local HTTP server allows importing playlists by scanning a QR code from another device.
  • Remote control — A local web interface lets you control playback from another device on the same network.
  • DLNA — Casting media to DLNA-compatible devices on your local network.

Permissions

  • Photo Library — Used only if you choose to import a playlist file from your device. No photos are accessed or uploaded.
  • Local Network — Used for QR import, remote control, and DLNA features as described above.
  • Background Audio — Allows audio playback to continue when the app is in the background.

Children's Privacy

VOXTV does not knowingly collect any data from children. The app includes a parental control feature with PIN protection.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
